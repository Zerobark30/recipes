Ingredients:
 - 3 eggs
 - 1 tbs unsalted butter
 - 2 tbs creme fraiche
 - salt
 - pepper
 - 1 tsp chopped chives
 
Directions:
 1. Place medium sauce pan on stove, turn to medium heat.
 
 2. When pan is nice and hot (~5 minutes, depending on stove) crack eggs in
	pan, add butter. begin stirring with spatula immediately. 
	DO NOT STOP STIRRING. EVER.
 
 3. As egg/butter mixture begins to steam, remove from heat. Continue to stir.
	After pan has cooled a bit (1-2 minutes) place the pan back on the heat
	and continue stirring. After another 3-4 minutes, remove it from the heat.
	Continue this process of putting pan on the stove and taking it off until
	eggs have solidified a little beyond your desired texture. Remember to keep
	stirring the whole time.
	
 4.	Remove the pan from the stove and add salt and pepper to taste as well as
	creme fraiche and chives, stirring one last time to mix everything up.
	Serve over thick toast, preferably sourdough.
